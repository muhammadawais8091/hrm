About Us

About Company

We, the Delta Global Consultants, are study abroad/ education consultants with more than ten years of experience helping students to reach academic goals. We provide students with the best opportunities to seek education in global academic institutions of higher learning, matching their academic interests and educational objectives.

Our Services

Based on required information furnished by students, we provide guidance on desired destination, field of study, culture, living costs, etc.

We encourage parents, sponsors, and other family members of students to join consulting sessions. Backed-up by a wide array of international expertise and knowledge, Delta Global Consultants will facilitate in providing students with useful information to take their next step in their educational and career endeavors.

Our Vision

To become an outstanding and finest educational consulting firm with an international flair to facilitate students in seeking admission opportunities to study abroad.
